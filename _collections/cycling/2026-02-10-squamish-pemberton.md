Two days. One night camping just past Callaghan Valley.
The road conditions were what you'd expect for February — wet, cold in
the mornings, but the mountains don't care about your comfort.

## The route

Squamish → Whistler via the Sea to Sky, then the back road down to
Pemberton. Return via D'Arcy road and back through the valley.
Roughly 180km total with about 2,400m of climbing.

The loaded bike makes the climbs honest. No way to pretend they're easy
when you're carrying camping gear, food for two days, and the usual
repair kit.

## What worked

The bag setup has been dialled for a while now — nothing shifting,
nothing rattling. The sleeping system was borderline but adequate.
Got down to about 2°C overnight and I slept fine.

## What I'd change

Start earlier. We lost an hour to a slow morning and spent the last
30km of day one riding in the dark. Not a disaster, but not the plan.

## Worth it

Yes. It always is.
